Field manual

Objective: first survivor to secure 10 serums wins. Each turn has two phases.

The dice

Every die shows one of three faces, two of each: a Botanist (they can extract serum), a Deathcap (a carnivorous fungus that eats your progress), or a Serum vial.

Phase 1 — Harvest

Roll a hand of 5. After every roll:

  • If the hand has at least 1 Botanist and 1 Serum, lock away min(Botanists, Serums) Serum dice — secured for Phase 2. Botanists stay in hand.
  • Otherwise, if Deathcaps outnumber Serums + Botanists in hand, the excess Deathcaps are discarded (no scoring effect).
  • If the hand ever goes all Deathcaps, the rot takes the field — Phase 1 ends immediately. Anything already secured carries forward.
  • Reroll what's left and repeat until only 1 die remains, then Phase 1 ends.

Phase 2 — Extraction

Roll a fresh hand of 5, once only:

  • Botanists and Serums together make up to min(Botanists, Serums) Serums lockable.
  • Total Serum = secured in Phase 1 + lockable from this roll.
  • Points this turn = Total Serum − Deathcaps in this roll. If Deathcaps outnumber your serums, the difference is subtracted from your score — but your score never drops below zero.

Console operation

Tap Roll to see the dice, then Resolve to lock in the outcome — every roll takes both taps. When Phase 2 resolves, hit Secure & pass to add the points to the active survivor and hand the breaker to the other one.

Versions — escalating infestation

Cycle with the V1/V2/V3 button (this restarts the current turn):

  • V1 — Outbreak: the classic die, 2 Botanist / 2 Deathcap / 2 Serum. Locks bank 1 serum each. The steady race.
  • V2 — Overgrowth: 1 Botanist / 3 Deathcap / 2 Serum, but every locked serum die banks 2. Bigger jackpots, nastier losses.
  • V3 — Full Bloom: 1 Botanist / 4 Deathcap / 1 Serum, and every locked serum die banks 4. Nearly half your turns will bleed serums — but a single lucky pairing can win the game outright. Pure chaos.
