Field manual

Objective: first survivor to secure 10 serums wins. Each turn has two phases. Drag the view to look around the grove.

The dice

Each die's six faces mix a Botanist (extracts serum), a Deathcap (a carnivorous fungus that eats your progress), and a Serum vial — the mix depends on the version.

Phase 1 — Harvest

Roll a hand of 5. After every roll:

  • If the hand has at least 1 Botanist and 1 Serum, lock away min(Botanists, Serums) Serum dice — secured for Phase 2. Botanists stay in hand.
  • Otherwise, if Deathcaps outnumber Serums + Botanists in hand, the excess Deathcaps are discarded (no scoring effect).
  • If the hand ever goes all Deathcaps, the rot takes the field — Phase 1 ends immediately. Anything already secured carries forward.
  • Reroll what's left and repeat until only 1 die remains, then Phase 1 ends.

Calling the chopper

After any resolved Harvest roll you may hit Call chopper to end Harvest immediately and move to Extraction. But loitering is dangerous: your first 3 Harvest rolls are safe — every roll beyond that adds +1 phantom deathcap to your Extraction count as the rot overruns the landing zone. Bank greedily or fly out clean; that's the game.

Phase 2 — Extraction

Roll a fresh hand of 5, once only:

  • Botanists and Serums together make up to min(Botanists, Serums) Serums lockable.
  • Total Serum = secured in Phase 1 + lockable from this roll (times the version's banking rate).
  • Points this turn = Total Serum − Deathcaps in this roll. If Deathcaps outnumber your serums, the difference is subtracted from your score — but your score never drops below zero.

Console operation

Tap Roll to throw the dice, then Resolve to lock in the outcome — every roll takes both taps. When Phase 2 resolves, hit Secure & pass to add the points to the active survivor and hand the breaker to the other one.

Versions — escalating infestation

  • Standard conditions: 1 Botanist / 3 Deathcap / 2 Serum faces, locks bank 2. Big jackpots, nasty losses.
  • Pandemic conditions: 1 Botanist / 4 Deathcap / 1 Serum, locks bank 4. Pure chaos — one lucky pairing can win the game outright.

☣ Pandemic mode

Every turn after the first has roughly a 2-in-7 chance the bloom surges: that turn is played under pandemic conditions — the deadlier die and the 4x banking rate — announced at the start of the turn, with tremors shaking the grove. It lasts one turn, then conditions settle.
